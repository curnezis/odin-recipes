Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This recipe is one I use as often as I can find time for! Chocolate chip cookies have a way of satisfying all of my cravings! These stay good for days (if they last that long!) but you can always freeze your dough and bake them whenever you want a lil snack :)

Ingredients

  • 2 sticks of Butter
  • 3/4 cup Sugar
  • 3/4 cuyp Brown Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 1/4 cups Flour
  • 12oz Chocolate Chips
  • Flakey Salt for finishing (optional)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. Cream together butter and sugar until pale and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, fully incorporating after each addition.
  4. Add vanilla and combine.
  5. In a separate bowl- combine flour, baking soda, and salt.
  6. Slowly add your flour mixture to the wet ingredients. Mix just until everything comes together.
  7. Add chocolate chips and stir.
  8. Bake for 15 minutes.
  9. Add flakey salt immediately after baking and enjoy!

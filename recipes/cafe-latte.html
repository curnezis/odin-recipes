Classic Cafe Latte

Description

This is a plain latte. I have a sensitive tummy so i use oatmilk but do what you wish. Add flavor if you want, it do be that easy.

Ingredients

  • 18 grams good Coffee beans whole
  • Milk of your choice (it better be oatmilk)

You will need an espresso machine so figure that out

Directions

  1. Weigh out your beans if you haven't and grind them on a fine setting into your portafilter.
  2. Distribute the ground coffee to remove any clumps.
  3. Tamp down the coffee with reasonable pressure, you want a perfectly flat surface.
  4. Lock into your espresso machine and brew, we aim for 36 grams of espresso in ~20-30 seconds.
  5. Steam your milk until barely too hot to touch.
  6. At this point you can add flavor or just the milk, but thats it!
